Matrix looks like this:

//  RED   GREEN    BLUE    YELLOW   <-- Attracted/Repelled BY:
    0.5f, -0.2f,   0.8f,   0.0f  , // RED reacts to...
    0.7f,  0.1f,  -0.4f,  -0.5f  , // GREEN reacts to...
   -0.3f,  0.6f,   0.2f,   0.1f  , // BLUE reacts to...
    0.2f, -0.8f,   0.0f,  -0.1f  ,  // YELLOW reacts to...
-force = repulsion
+force = attraction
